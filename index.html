About

What is TED?

TED is a global nonprofit, devoted to "ideas worth spreading", founded in 1984. It began as a conference where Technology Entertainment and Design converged, but now cover almost all topics from science to social issues in more than 100 different languages. The TED foundation believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives, and ultimately, the world.

What is TEDx?

TEDx was created in the spirit of TED’s mission, “ideas worth spreading”. It supports independent organizers who want to create a TED- like event in their own community. At TEDx events, screening of TED Talk videos as well as local speakers take the stage to initiate thoughtful action and stimulate meaningful dialogue at the local level.

What is TEDxShivajicollege?

Shivaji College is proud to receive a licence from TED to organise TEDx event on our campus. By organising a TEDx event, we will come closer to the mission of the college - "transform one life, transform the nation." We believe, these sessions will not only inspire the Shivaji College community but also join ranks with other communities in organising such life-changing programmes. Only a handful of colleges in Delhi University conducts TEDx so far.
